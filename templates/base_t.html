About us

The only way to make money from trading, be it in property, equity, forex or sports is to identify value! But unlike other trading markets, tennis is not influenced by economic factors and only reliant on the skills and mental strength of the two players on court, and therefore data driven strategies can have larger effects on returns.

And that is exactly what TennisBrain does. There is no point trading on matches where the outcome has a 90% chance of occurring if the market is pricing it at 1.05 (95%). You may win 9 times out of 10, but the 1 time you lose will move you into a losing position. Identifying value accurately is the key to making returns on tennis.

We use data-led algorithms to predict over 90% of ATP and WTA Singles Tour tennis matches, providing users with the world's most sophisticated insight on betting markets. Utilising millions of historical data points (and growing by the day), our model compiles data on a huge range of key attributes, identifying hidden advantages to accurately predict the outcome of any given match and isolate value from betting markets. From height, form, age, surface, head to heads, games and sets played previously, to even average service speed in the last 3 months, we have it covered.

Consistently achieving over a 13% return across recommended trades, TennisBrain adds the power of data to tennis trading.
